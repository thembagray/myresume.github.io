Being You: Check out the app here

Overview

The primary objective of BeingYou is to empower students by offering a range

of mental health resources and tools conveniently accessible through their

smartphones. By integrating features such as scheduling sessions with school

therapists, text, voice, and video chat capabilities, as well as soothing music playlists

for relaxation, BeingYou strives to create a supportive environment conducive to

students' well-being.


Design Challenges

One of the key design challenges encountered during the development of BeingYou

was ensuring seamless integration of a third-party system for video calls while

maintaining compatibility with the app's existing codebase. Additionally, stringent

security protocols were implemented to safeguard user data and ensure compliance

with privacy regulations

Features

Therapist Sessions: schedule appointments and engage in text, voice, or video calls with school therapists

Music for Relaxation: playlists of calming music to aid relaxation, improve sleep quality, and alleviate stress and anxiety.

Breathing Exercises: Interactive breathing exercises

Diary: keep a digital diary to track their thoughts, feelings, and experiences, promoting self-reflection and emotional awareness
